Frequently Asked

Common Questions

How long does a typical roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements in the Grand Rapids area take one to two days from tear-off to cleanup, depending on size and complexity. We provide a clear written timeline in every estimate.

Do you install roofs through Michigan winters?

Yes. Asphalt shingles can be installed year-round when handled properly. We adjust technique for cold weather, including hand-sealing tabs when needed. If conditions on a given day are not right for a quality install, we say so and reschedule.

What warranty do you offer?

Installations are backed by manufacturer warranties from Atlas (we are Atlas Pro Gold certified) and our own workmanship warranty. Specific terms are written into every estimate.

How much does a new roof cost in Grand Rapids?

Cost depends on square footage, pitch, materials, and tear-off complexity. After a free in-person assessment, you receive a written estimate with no hidden fees. Financing is available through Medallion Bank, Lake Michigan Credit Union, and Michigan Saves.

Do you tear off the old roof or overlay it?

We tear off and inspect the roof deck on nearly every replacement. Overlays mask underlying problems and shorten the life of the new roof. The honest answer is almost always full tear-off.

How do I know if I need a repair versus a full replacement?

A free assessment is the only way to know for sure. Age of the existing roof, granule loss, active leaks, attic ventilation, and deck condition all factor in. We give you the honest answer, including when a repair will buy you several more years.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Simpatico Roofing & Consulting is fully licensed and insured in the State of Michigan. We are also Atlas Pro Gold certified and a Michigan Saves Authorized Contractor (#204229).
